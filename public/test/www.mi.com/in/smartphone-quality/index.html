Smartphone Quality

We are committed to provide the best quality at an honest price. Our phones go through a rigorous 10-point quality check, so you receive nothing but the best.

Watch video

  • Drop Test

    Dropped more than a 100 times at multiple angles from a distance of 1m to ensure durability and resistance.

    Learn more

  • Temperature Test

    Subjected to temperatures ranging from -20°C to 60°C to make sure the phone continues to perform in different temperature conditions.

    Learn more

  • Keylife Test

    Buttons pressed 5 Lakh times, applying multiple modes of pressure, so the buttons don't stop working after repeated usage.

    Learn more

  • SAR Test

    Thorough tests conducted to prove the Specific Absorption Rate (SAR) level is well within the recommended standard.

    Learn more

  • Touchscreen Test

    Touch panel subjected to tests on all 4 corners and the mid points of the screen so the touch is operational in any condition.

    Learn more

  • Squeeze Test

    Device subjected to a pressure of 250N, so the device is capable of sustaining certain amount of pressure.

    Learn more

  • Connector Durability Test

    USB, headphones, tray and battery covers are removed and reinserted thousands of times to make sure the detachable attachments are strong.

    Learn more

  • Vibration Motor Test

    Vibration motor put on 72 hours of continuous work without suspension to ensure device vibration is unhindered
