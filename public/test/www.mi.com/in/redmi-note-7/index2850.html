Redmi Note 7 Frequently Asked Questions:


  • Can I use my wired headphones with the Redmi Note 7?

    Yes, Redmi Note 7 comes with a 3.5mm headphone jack, which allows you to use your earphones/headphones with the phone.


  • Does the phone charge quickly?

    Yes, Redmi Note 7 supports Quick Charge 4.0 (adapter sold separately).



  • Should I put a screen guard on the screen?

    The display comes with Corning Gorilla Glass 5 which protects the display against day to day scratches. You could opt for a screen guard for added protection.



  • Can I shoot portrait photos on the front camera and rear?

    Yes, there is a portrait mode on the 13MP front camera and the dual rear camera setup of 12MP+2MP for you to capture elegant portrait shots.



  • Can I shoot pictures in different scenes on the Redmi Note 7?

    Yes, Redmi Note 7 comes with AI scene detection that detects multiple scenes on both the front and rear to create better images.



  • What version of Android does it have?

    Redmi Note 7 runs MIUI 10 based on Android 9.0.



  • Can I shoot high-quality videos?

    Yes, you can shoot Full HD videos on the device.



  • Does it have image stabilization of any kind?

    Yes, there’s Electronic Image Stabilisation to allow for jerk-free videos.



  • Can I use two apps on the same screen?

    Yes, using the split screen option, you can use two apps at once.



  • Does it have face unlock?

    Yes, it has AI face unlock. Redmi Note 7 has a fingerprint sensor on the back, too.



  • Can I hide the Dot Notch?

    Yes, you can hide the notch in the ‘Full-Screen Display’ option in Settings



  • How much battery life can I expect?

    With a 4000mAh battery, you can expect up to a two-day battery depending upon the usage.



  • What’s the secondary camera on the rear used for?

    The secondary camera is a depth sensor used for getting depth information for objects in the background.



  • Does the front camera has LED flash?

    There is no hardware flash, but Redmi Note 7 has an intelligent screen flash to take selfies in low light.



  • Is the phone a waterproof phone?

    Redmi Note 7 has a special P2i nano-coating that makes it splash resistant (not to be confused as waterproof).
